About Me

  • Post by Bethany
  • Jan 01, 2020

I began my career as an in-home childcare provider in 2013. I traveled around the world as a short-term au pair in four different countries for a little over two years, and then landed in California when I came back to the USA, where I have had the pleasure of working with two different families for longer periods of time.

On this site you’ll be able to find a lot of information about my childcare experience and philosophy, but if you’d like to learn more about me and my interests, this is the place!

I enjoy keeping myself busy and active. I spend my weekends hiking, rock climbing, and paragliding. On more relaxing weekends, a well-stocked cheese board and a few board games are a nice activity with friends. I’m always looking forward to new experiences, as well - a pottery wheel class, archery, or salsa dance night at a local cafe. I studied music therapy at university, and while I’ve gotten out of practice with all the other activities I’ve taken up lately, I play french horn, as well as a bit of piano and guitar. I am always in the middle of a book and enjoy completing a yearly reading prompt challenge. I enjoy writing in my journal, and it’s fun to occasionally read old ones to take a trip down memory lane. I keep an assortment of craft supplies on hand for making cards or adding an extra splash of color to my journals. I am trying to improve my drawing skills, but so far can only copy coloring book-style outlines. Though they live in Ohio, I am close with my family; we video call at least once a week to keep in touch. I listen to all sorts of music, from classical concertos to 60’s and 70’s rock to country, but my favorite is Broadway showtunes. I love Halloween, and will usually start at the end of summer scouring thrift shops to piece together a costume (this year, I am starting to think about Jasmine.) I love to cook and am constantly flipping through cookbooks and browsing Pinterest to expand my collection of tried-and-true recipes.

Please don’t hesitate to contact me if you have any questions. I am looking forward to getting to know you and your family!
